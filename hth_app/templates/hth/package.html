Overview

Known as Greece's "co-capital," Thessaloniki is a captivating destination that blends history, culture, and vibrant city life. It is the capital of Central Macedonia and it is known for its natural beauty and rich historical importance.
Thessaloniki has a rich past which dates back over 2,300 years and history lovers will encounter magnificent remnants of the Byzantine, Ottoman, and Roman eras. The White Tower is an iconic symbol of the city and it stands as a distinguished landmark of Thessaloniki representing the sole surviving remnant of the once-demolished Ottoman fortifications that surrounded the city.
The city pulses with energy, thanks to its lively markets, vibrant nightlife, and welcoming locals and it is filled with cafes, boutiques, and stunning neoclassical buildings.
Art and culture thrive in Thessaloniki, with a plethora of museums, galleries, and theaters to explore while the city's local cousine will transport you to a world of gastronomic bliss.
Thessaloniki's warm climate and Mediterranean charm make it a year-round destination for tourists that seek a delightful escape. Discover the magic of this city and create memories that will last a lifetime. Book your trip to Thessaloniki now and let the adventure begin!
